Eligibility requirements

To join Real Broker BC as a brand new licensee, you must meet these minimum requirements.

1) BCFSA first-time license

You must have a First Time License issued by BCFSA.

RegulatoryBCFSA

2) Applied Practice Course started

You must have completed a portion of the Applied Practice Course (APC).

EducationAPC

3) Mentor Agreement in place

Mentor Agreement must be completed through onboarding before supervised assignments.

OnboardingCompliance

How the program works

A straightforward, supervised path from new license to confident, compliant production.

Mentor responsibilities

Your Mentor is a local experienced Real agent who supports you while you complete Brokerage-supervised APC assignments.

  • Supervise and guide APC supervised assignments
  • Provide transaction guidance and professional development
  • Support compliance and best practices

Agent responsibilities

You’re still the operator. The Mentor is the guardrail.

  • Complete APC requirements on schedule
  • Use mentor guidance for transactions + compliance questions
  • Follow Real Broker and BCFSA standards

FAQ

Keep this short and blunt. New agents don’t read walls of text.

Do I need a mentor to join Real Broker BC as a new licensee?

If you are a first-time licensee completing the Applied Practice Course with Brokerage-supervised assignments, yes — you need a Mentor Agreement completed through onboarding.

Who completes the Mentor Agreement?

Onboarding coordinates the paperwork. You and the Mentor align on expectations and supervision requirements.

When does mentorship end?

When the APC supervised requirements are satisfied and onboarding confirms completion.

Where do I find Real logos and official brand colors?

In Real’s Brand Elements portal / Marketing Center (approved logos and official color swatches). See: https://support.therealbrokerage.com/hc/en-us/articles/16488129375639-Where-can-I-find-the-Real-logos and https://support.therealbrokerage.com/hc/en-us/articles/19610278858007-What-are-Real-s-Brand-Colors

Can I start writing offers immediately?

You should follow the supervision expectations set out in your Mentor Agreement and use your mentor as the compliance guardrail. Don’t guess.

Who do I contact if I’m stuck?

Start with your Mentor. If it’s an onboarding / agreement issue, contact onboarding.
